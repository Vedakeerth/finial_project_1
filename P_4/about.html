About us

Vaelinsa was founded in 2023 with a mission to revolutionize the 3D printing industry. We are a leading manufacturer of 3D printing machines and offer top-quality services to our clients. Our goal is to provide innovative, reliable, and affordable 3D printing technology to individuals and businesses of all sizes. At Vaelinsa, we understand the importance of staying ahead of the curve in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape. That's why we invest heavily in research and development to ensure that our 3D printers are always at the forefront of innovation. We use the latest materials and technologies to create machines that are precise, versatile, and easy to use. Our printers are capable of printing a wide variety of materials, including plastics & metals.
