What is Product Design & Development ?

    Product design and development in 3D printing involves the creation and optimization of digital models for the purpose of 3D printing physical products. It is the process of designing and developing a product that can be produced using 3D printing technology.

    The process begins with the creation of a digital model or design of the product, which can be done using computer-aided design (CAD) software or other modeling tools. The design is then optimized for 3D printing, taking into account factors such as material properties, print resolution, and other production requirements.
